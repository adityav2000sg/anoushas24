# Sound Effects

Add these sound files to enable audio feedback:

## Required Files
- `confetti.mp3` - Party popper/confetti explosion (plays on intro)
- `drawer.mp3` - Wooden drawer sliding sound (plays when opening wishes)
- `shake.mp3` - Jar shaking/rattling sound (plays when shaking the jar)
- `pop.mp3` - Bubble pop sound (plays when note appears)
- `success.mp3` - Chime/success sound (optional, for completion)

## Where to Find Free Sounds
- [Mixkit](https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/) - Free, no attribution required
- [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/) - Free, no attribution required  
- [Freesound](https://freesound.org/) - Free, check individual licenses

## Recommended Sounds from Mixkit
- Confetti: Search "party popper" or "celebration"
- Drawer: Search "drawer" or "sliding wood"
- Shake: Search "rattle" or "shake"
- Pop: Search "pop" or "bubble"
- Success: Search "success" or "chime"

## Tips
- Keep files small (under 100KB each)
- MP3 format works best
- Short sounds (under 2 seconds) feel snappier
